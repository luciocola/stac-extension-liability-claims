== Outlook 

Future development of the STAC Liability and Claims Extension will focus on deeper integration with evolving geospatial and semantic web ecosystems, enhanced automation capabilities, and expanded domain-specific support.

**STAC API Extensions**: Development of query and filtering extensions specifically for liability claims, including temporal queries on claim lifecycle events, spatial queries on affected areas, and complex provenance graph traversal.

**Machine Learning Integration**: Expansion of quality metadata to support ML model training datasets with automated quality assessment pipelines, model provenance tracking, and training data lineage documentation.

**Blockchain and Distributed Ledger Integration**: Investigation of immutable provenance records using blockchain technology, with focus on legal admissibility and long-term archival guarantees.

**Real-time Streaming Support**: Extension of metadata model to support streaming STAC catalogs with live liability claim updates, enabling real-time incident tracking and continuous quality monitoring.

**Visualization Tools**: Development of reference implementations for provenance graph visualization, quality dashboard generation, and interactive claim management interfaces.

The extension's modular architecture and strong standards foundation position it well for these evolutionary enhancements while maintaining backward compatibility with existing implementations.
